  • About

    • Well, it exists.

  • Controls

    • On ground

    • Mouse for looking around
    • W, A, S, D for walking
    • Space for deploying or undeploying glider
    • F3 for increasing wind
    • F2 for decreasing wind
    • Esc to quit

    In air

    • Mouse to look around in first-person view
    • A for left brake
    • D for right brake
    • W for speed system
    • Q to lean left
    • E to lean right
    • V to toggle between 1st- and 3rd-person view

  • Features

    • Full ground character control
    • 3D models for glider and harness
    • Glider controls as braking, speeding, weight shifting
    • Realistic wind with custom wind velocity and direction
    • Ridge soar effects
    • A smalls scene with mountains
